Meghan Rennie

Meghan Makes Music!

Meghan has been writing and performing music since she was a teenager, originally working under the project name Be Kind to Dogs. She's since switched to using her own name. She keeps promising that she'll make better-quality recordings of her songs and release them "officially"; in the meantime, a selection of her work can be found dispersed across various online platforms.

  • Soundcloud
  • Bandcamp
  • YouTube

Meghan also created the into/outro music for the podcast Excuses to Connect by Richard Lee-Thai, which can be accessed wherever you listen to podcasts.
